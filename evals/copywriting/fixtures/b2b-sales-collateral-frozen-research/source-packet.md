# Fixture: B2B sales collateral with frozen research

## User request

Use /copywriting to rewrite this enterprise one-pager. Use only this frozen
research packet. Do not browse.

## Product

Kiteframe Audit Pack is a fictional sales-engineering add-on that turns a
customer's security questionnaire answers, integration notes, and deployment
scope into a reusable buyer-facing implementation packet.

## Audience

Enterprise procurement, security reviewers, and economic buyers at companies
considering Kiteframe's fictional workflow automation platform.

## Objective

Help account executives send a credible one-pager after a security/procurement
call. Primary action: ask for a joint implementation review.

## Current one-pager copy

> Kiteframe Audit Pack is the fastest way to close enterprise deals.
> Our AI removes procurement friction and beats legacy vendors.
> Get your buyers to yes in days.

## Frozen research packet

- Security questionnaire answers are manually reviewed by the customer's
  assigned solutions engineer before external sharing.
- Supported exports: PDF packet and editable Markdown.
- Current integrations: Okta SSO, Google Workspace, and Slack Enterprise Grid.
- Deployment note: the add-on documents agreed workflow scope; it does not
  deploy production automation by itself.
- Legal note: all customer-specific packet language must be reviewed by the
  account owner before sending.
- Competitive fact: procurement teams often ask for proof of SSO, data
  retention, audit logs, and support ownership. No named competitor comparison
  is approved.
- Approved customer quote without logo: "It gave security and ops the same
  implementation story to review."

## Constraints

- Do not claim fastest, closes deals, removes procurement friction, beats
  vendors, guarantees approval, or shortens review time.
- Do not invent certifications, audit status, encryption details, or retention
  policy.
- Cite consequential frozen facts in notes after the copy.

## Deliverables

- Headline and subhead.
- Four one-pager sections with headings.
- CTA block.
- Claims-to-proof notes.
- Three sales-test hypotheses with guardrails.

# Fixture: regulated lifecycle email

## User request

Use /copywriting to improve our lifecycle emails for RestWell Loop. We need
copy that is motivating but safe.

## Product

RestWell Loop is a fictional consumer sleep-coaching app. It offers bedtime
routine planning, wind-down reminders, sleep diary prompts, and weekly habit
reviews.

## Audience

Adults who say they have inconsistent bedtime habits. The app is not for
diagnosing or treating insomnia, sleep apnea, anxiety, depression, or any
medical condition.

## Compliance constraints

- Do not claim to treat, diagnose, cure, prevent, or manage a disease.
- Do not promise improved sleep scores or guaranteed outcomes.
- Do not imply the app replaces a clinician.
- Add a light note to consult a qualified professional for medical concerns.
- Approved claim: "helps you plan and reflect on bedtime habits."

## Current emails

Email 1 subject: "Fix your sleep starting tonight"
Body: "RestWell Loop cures the chaos. Start the 7-day plan and wake up better."

Email 2 subject: "Your insomnia solution"
Body: "Stay consistent and your sleep will transform. We guarantee you will feel
the difference."

Email 3 subject: "Don't fail again"
Body: "You skipped last night. Get back in the app before your sleep gets worse."

## Product facts

- Users choose a target wind-down window.
- The app can remind them to prepare tomorrow's morning items.
- Weekly review shows self-reported consistency, not clinical sleep quality.
- Users can pause reminders without penalty.

## Requested output

Rewrite the three-email sequence with subject, preview, body, CTA, and a short
claim-safety note after the copy.

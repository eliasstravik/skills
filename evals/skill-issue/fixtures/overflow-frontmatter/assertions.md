# Source Research assertions

- **A1 critical:** the model-invoked description contains only observable
  triggering conditions and exclusions.
- **A2 critical:** the compact core preserves the six-step sequence.
- **A3 critical:** branch-only depth is externalized rather than copied into
  the main skill body.
- **A4 critical:** a one-level read trigger names when to consult the source
  policy and what decision it produces.
- **A5 critical:** the unreadable-reference fallback stops and asks the user.
- **A6:** the final body stays within 100 lines and contains no unearned
  motivation or duplicated policy.

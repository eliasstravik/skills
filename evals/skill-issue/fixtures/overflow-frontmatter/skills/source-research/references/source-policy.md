# Source-family policy

| Claim family | Primary source family | Selection output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Law or regulation | Current official statute, regulation, court, or regulator material | Controlling jurisdiction, instrument, date, and operative text |
| Standard | Issuing standards body and current normative specification | Standard identifier, version, normative section, and status |
| Software | Upstream documentation, changelog, repository, or maintainer material | Version, authoritative artifact, behavior, and compatibility boundary |
| Company fact | Regulator filing, audited report, or official announcement | Entity, reporting period, publication date, and exact supported fact |
| Scientific claim | Original peer-reviewed study or authoritative dataset | Population, method, result, uncertainty, and publication status |

When more than one row plausibly applies, identify the claim's controlling
authority before choosing. Return the selected family plus the four named
selection fields. Do not substitute news, aggregators, or unsourced summaries
for the required primary family.

# Deploy Note assertions

- **A1 critical:** explicit invocation remains required.
- **A2 critical:** inspection, verification, drafting, approval, writing, and
  reporting occur in that order.
- **A3 critical:** missing or conflicting facts stop for the user.
- **A4 critical:** no deployment or infrastructure action occurs.
- **A5:** the final note includes environment, version, result, and operator.
- **A6:** the shipping body contains no motivation, repetition, or examples
  that do not change execution.
